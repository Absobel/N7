Kyou wa 'Ninniku to Komugiko to Gyuunyuu no Shiifoodo Pasuta' o goshoukai shimasu

Décrire un plat:

1. Kono ryouri wa totemo oishii desu.
2. Kono ryouri wa karai desu.
3. Komugiko to ninniku o tsukatta ryouri wa amai desu.
4. Gyokai ga takusan haitte ite, aji ga koi desu.
5. Shiro wain o tsukatta ryouri wa sappari to shita aji ga shimasu.
6. Kono ryouri wa aburakkokute, omoi desu.
7. Bataa to gyuunyuu de tsukutta ryouri wa maroyaka na aji ga shimasu.
8. Kono kiji no ryouri wa sukoshi shibui desu.

Préparation de la recette:

1. Mazu, ninniku o muite kudasai.
2. Sorekara, komugiko o sukoshi mazete kudasai.
3. Tsugi ni, gyuunyuu o yudete kudasai.
4. Bataa o irete, atatamete kudasai.
5. Gyokai o kizande kudasai.
6. Sorekara, gyokai o itamete kudasai.
7. Shiro wain o sukoshi kuwaete kudasai.
8. Aji o tsukeru tame ni, shio o tsukatte kudasai.
9. Saigo ni, subete no zairyou o mazete, yukkuri to niru kudasai.
10. Ryouri ga dekitara, aratte kara tabete kudasai.
11. Kono ryouri wa hiyashite mo oishii node, koorasete mo ii desu yo.
12. Ryouri o tsukuru mae ni, te o aratte kudasai.
13. Ryouri o tsukuru toki ni, kirei na utsuwa o tsukatte kudasai.
14. Kogenai you ni, ryouri o yoku mite kudasai.
15. Amari ni mo karakunaranai you ni, koushinryou o sukoshi shika tsukawanaide kudasai.
16. Gyuunyuu ga nietara, hi o tomete kudasai.
17. Ryouri o oetara, subete no dougu o aratte kudasai.
18. Tsukutta ryouri o reizouko de hozon shite kudasai.
19. Tsuginohi niwa, reizouko kara ryouri o toridashi, shikkari tokashite kudasai.
20. Ryouri o saido atatamete, oishiku meshiagatte kudasai.
21. Yoi shokuji o!
